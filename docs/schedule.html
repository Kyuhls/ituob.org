Schedule an edition

Before you start, make sure you know the cutoff and publication dates for the edition you’ll be scheduling.

  1. Open OB calendar by clicking “Schedule” button in main application window.

    You will see calendar on the left, and a list of OB editions on the right. The month selected in the calendar determines which OB editions are shown.

  2. In the calendar, use arrows or dropdowns to select the year and month corresponding to the desired cutoff date.

  3. Click on the day corresponding to the desired cutoff date to start scheduling the edition.

  4. Click on the day corresponding to the desired publication date for the edition being scheduled. If needed, use the arrows to switch to the next month.

  5. On the right, enter the numeric ID of the edition you’re adding.

  6. Confirm that publication and cutoff dates are correct. If you need to edit either date, click the pencil button next to it and select a new date in the calendar.

  7. Click Save to save edition schedule.
