step: Add Lists Annexed message. directions: - step: | In the beginning of the OB edition file you downloaded, find the “Lists annexed…” section header. - step: | On the left side of the Issue Editor window in ITU OB Editor app, click the plus button below the “General Messages” header and select the “Lists Annexed” menu item. notes: - text: | Message editor screen will appear on the right side of the window. icon: fas fa-info-circle intent: primary - step: | Check that the items under Running Annexes tab in the message editor, and items under A. list in the legacy edition, are the same (disregarding the order they appear in). notes: - This assumes you have found, downloaded and opened a legacy edition in MS Word or PDF format, and that you have the ITU OB Editor app opened & configured.
